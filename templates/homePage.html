Mission Statement

Currently, climate change data is restricted to technical users capable of creating specific scripts that translate data from various APIs to readable files. Our vision: a world where accessible, customized, and powerful climate insights empower users to make informed decisions.

Why did we create ClimaTech?

While most people understand that climate change is a growing issue for our generation, thereâ€™s still a misconception regarding how quickly our actions manifest into real-time consequences. Furthermore, relevant climate data is typically locked behind technical skill requirements and API usage.

What is our solution?

Our solution provides a convenient and interactive method for users to identify predicted temperatures/temperature variance. With the use of customizable features, we allow users to obtain an overview of specific locations over a range of 15 years which allows YOU to research what YOU'RE interested in.
